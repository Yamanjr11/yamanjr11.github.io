Introduction about Expert tranning

Experts Training is an arena for developing IT professionals in Nepal. It is a knowledge sharing place where professionals working in different technologies will share ideas and experiences by conducting free seminars with those who want to develop career in IT. We run special short course called experts advice when you need to learn a specific technology in short span of time. We also conduct professional programming training run by the experts (Senior Programmers, Project Managers, CSS designers, etc) who have several years of practical experience

If you are a student or an IT professional seeking for quality training for your skill/knowledge enhancement, please check our instructor profiles so that you can be assured for the investment you are doing.

If you are an IT company and you want to hire professionally trained trainees geared up with latest IT knowledge and skill, please check our trainee profiles.
